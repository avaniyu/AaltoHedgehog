Neighborhood car is a shared electric car for your neighborhood

How it works

How it works



Book

Book a free time slot or join a neighbor's booking through the mobile app.

Pick up

Go to the car and unlock it with the app, no need to pick up a car key.

Drive

Enjoy the ride! When you’re done, just return the car to its parking spot.

Why choose Neighborhood car

Flexible

Use and pay for the car only for the time you actually need it.

Electric

Eco-friendly driving with zero emissions. No need to visit a gas station after using the car!

Affordable

Earn free driving time by cleaning the car. You can also share the car bookings with your neighbors to split the costs.

Nearby

We partner with housing providers, ensuring that the car is located in your neighbourhood garage or parking lot.

Pricing
